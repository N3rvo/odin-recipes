  • Caipirinha

Time to honour my roots and finally show something you can ingest.

You will need:

  • A lemon
  • Two tablespoons of sugar
  • Cachaça
  • Some ice

Now you just have to follow the steps:

  1. Slice both of the tips of the lemon, then slice it vertically and take the central axis (it is called columela by the way) out. Now cut it in strips.
  2. Put the lemon strips and the sugar into a cup and press the juice out of the lemon with a muddler.
  3. Put the ice into the glass and fill the rest of it with cachaça.
  4. Mix it up nicely with a spoon or a shaker.

Now it is ready to be served! Drinky responsibly.

Preparation Time: 5 min.
