This is one of my favourites! It involves doing that thing you can do in video-games: Running!

It is pretty easy:

  1. Find a suitable place to run by talking with friends or searching the internet.
  2. Choose a day to start running (it would be better if it is not a day far away, but that is up to you!)
  3. Gear up and go to the place.
  4. Warm up nicely.
  5. It's time to start, make a stride with either of your feet.
  6. Now it gets tricky, make a smilar stride with the foot that was left behind.
  7. Repeat the lest step for as much time as you want.

You are doing it! You are running, enjoy the ride!

Preparation Time: the time it takes your spirit ajust.
